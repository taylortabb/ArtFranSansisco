Art Fran Sansisco is an annual grant for artists in San Francisco's Mission District. Our grants are specifically focused on funding supplies, tools, and materials for the artist rather than specific projects.

Each year we grant three $500 awards for early to mid-career artists residing or working in The Mission. Our 2022 grant applications are due December 1st. Grantees will be notified of their award by December 15th. Applications are considered on the following rubric:

Criteria Weight
Personal Response Questions 5 Points
Portfolio 3 Points
Years Living/Working in Mission +1 Point Per Year
Our grant application is minimal and should take no longer than 15 minutes to complete. Submissions are welcome in any language, but English or Spanish are preffered.
Art Fran Sansisco is a 501c3 Organization through Powerhour Funding Co.
